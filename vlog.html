Day One

The New Jersey Primary elections are in 7 days. We are going to spend the next seven days showing you exactly what we are working on, how we are getting our message across, and how we are spending the funds that we raised to run for office.

Our goal is to make it clear that working people, people from different backgrounds, and people of all ages CAN run effective campaigns for public office and they should. We need to make it clear that money can't buy our elections. We can win by getting people involved and sharing information in an easy to digest way. We want to inspire more people across our country to pursue public office and run creative campaigns that work towards inspiring actions.

It can't be about who has the most money. We can win this, all we need as your help to spread the word.

Stay tuned for regular updates over the next ten days.
